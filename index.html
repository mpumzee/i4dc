About Us

We are a research and development communication consultancy established in 2018 offering a wide range of services to the development and corporate sector in South Africa We believe data, knowledge and technology are key drivers in problem solving and social transformation. Creative thinking and co-creation is the foundation of is our motto - Connecting Ideas.

Our Projects

The following are our current projects

Dev Talk

This is an upcoming documentary series that tackles ‘development’ issues in South Africa Broadcast once a month through the YouTube Channel and selected platforms, the programme interrogates development issues in South Africa, igniting debates on the development policies, approaches and interventions. It promotes sharing of new knowledge, identification of development gaps and proposition of alternative solutions.

The Narrative Podcast

This is a bi-monthly podcast that covers development issues in South Africa. It unpacks stories of change, interrogates development policies and approaches, gives a voice to the marginalised and brings together ideas to influence social transformation.
